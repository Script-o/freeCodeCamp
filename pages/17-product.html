Dungeons and Dragons Mini's

Able to paint miniatures for tabletop gaming with a large emphasis on hitting small details and using many colors to make your mini's pop. The shading and highlighting techniques used for single characters is tailored so that every color has its own shade and highlight to make sure every object on the miniature stands out.

Skirmish Games

Able to paint miniatures for skirmish games with a emphasis on unit cohesion and using between 3 and 8 colors as an overall theme to your army. The shading and highlighting techniques used for skirmish painting will hit general areas with similar shading and highlighting. This will give an effect of the units being lit by a similar light source when grouped together.

Wargaming

Able to paint miniatures for wargaming with an emphasis on batch painting and a good overall look to your whole army. The shading and highlighting techniques used for army painting are designed to give your overall army a professional look even while using the same shading and highlighting across the whole model.
